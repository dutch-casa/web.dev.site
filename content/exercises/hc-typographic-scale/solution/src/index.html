The Power of Typography

A well-designed typographic scale creates visual hierarchy and rhythm throughout your content.

Understanding Type Scale

A typographic scale is a progression of font sizes that work harmoniously together. By using a consistent ratio, you create a predictable and pleasing visual hierarchy.

Common Scale Ratios

Popular ratios include the golden ratio (1.618), major third (1.25), and perfect fourth (1.333). Each creates a different feel and spacing.

Implementation Details

Using CSS custom properties makes it easy to maintain and adjust your type scale across your entire project.

Best Practices

Keep line-height proportional to font size. Larger text typically needs less line-height, while body text benefits from 1.5-1.6.

Additional Resources

Tools like Type Scale and Modular Scale can help you calculate and visualize your typographic system.
