Understanding Web Accessibility
Published on
March 15, 2025
By Sarah Chen
Web accessibility ensures that websites and applications are usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. It's not just about compliance with standards; it's about creating inclusive digital experiences.
When we design with accessibility in mind, we make better products for all users. Features like keyboard navigation, proper heading structure, and descriptive link text benefit everyone, not just users with disabilities.
The key to accessible web development is understanding that accessibility is not a feature to add at the end, but a fundamental principle to guide every decision from the start.
